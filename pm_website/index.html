Project Overview

The Astana Census Project is dedicated to collecting up-to-date demographic data to support the sustainable growth and development of Astana. With cutting-edge technology, MAYBA offers insights that empower policymakers and urban planners to create a thriving future for the city.

Our Mission

At MAYBA, our mission is to gather accurate demographic data to inform policies that shape Astana’s growth. We believe in the power of data to transform cities and improve the lives of their residents.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

We use online and offline channels to collect census data, ensuring inclusivity. Residents can participate online, or be visited by trained interviewers.

2. Data Analysis

Our team processes the data using advanced analytics to extract meaningful insights that reflect Astana’s dynamic demographic landscape.

3. Report Generation

The final report is created, providing comprehensive information for urban planning and policy-making.
